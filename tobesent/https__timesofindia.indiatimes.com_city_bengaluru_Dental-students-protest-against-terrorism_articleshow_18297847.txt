Dental students protest against terrorism
TNN | Sep 19, 2001, 11.06 PM IST
davanagere: the students of two local dental colleges here took out a procession against world terrorism here on sunday and urged for world peace and harmony. a release said the students were upset and were concerned that the world was progressing towards terrorism as shown in the attack last tuesday on both wtc of new york and the pentagon in washington. a procession was taken out with cutouts bearing slogans against world terrorism and the procession culminated after an oath-taking against world terrorism.
