Naxalite explode landmine at Rudaram
Aug 16, 2001, 02.55 PM IST
jagdalpur: naxalites on thursday exploded a landmine at village rudaram under the bijapur police district where a police party had gone to remove the black flag hoisted by naxalites on wednesday on independence day. however, no one was injured, police sources said. soon after the six-member police party reached rudaram on thursday, naxalites exploded a landmine and fired several bullets, the sources said. bijapur police district was formally carved out from dantewada district on wednesday.
